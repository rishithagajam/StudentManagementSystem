## Our Pledge
We, the contributors and maintainers of the **Student Management System**, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free and inclusive experience for everyone.  
We are committed to providing an environment where individuals are treated with respect, regardless of:

- Age  
- Body size  
- Disability  
- Ethnicity or nationality  
- Gender identity or expression  
- Level of experience or education  
- Socio-economic background  
- Personal appearance  
- Race or religion  
- Sexual identity or orientation  

We strive to contribute to a community that is open, welcoming, and supportive.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:
- Showing empathy and kindness to other contributors  
- Being respectful toward differing opinions and feedback  
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them  
- Focusing on the best interest of the project and community  
- Giving constructive, helpful feedback  

### Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Sexualized language, images, or inappropriate advances  
- Harassment, abuse, insults, or derogatory comments  
- Personal or political attacks  
- Public or private threats or intimidation  
- Publishing othersâ€™ personal information without consent  
- Any conduct inappropriate for a professional environment

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers of the **Student Management System** project are responsible for:

- Clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing and addressing reports of misconduct  
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions violating this Code  
- Taking fair and proportional corrective action  
- Communicating reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate  

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, comments)  
- Public interactions where a contributor represents the project (online or offline)

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Minor, unintentional offense.  
**Consequence:** Private warning with clarification.

---

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** A violation or repeated minor misconduct.  
**Consequence:** Formal warning, temporary limits on communication.

---

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious violation or repeated behavior.  
**Consequence:** Temporary ban from public/private interactions with the project.

---

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Continuous violations or targeted harassment.  
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from community participation.
